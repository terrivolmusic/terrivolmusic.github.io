// ABOUT TERRIVOL MUSIC

More than a label.
An infrastructure
for sound.

Terrivol Music was born in Bogotá, Colombia in 2010 with a clear mission: build the infrastructure that independent artists need to compete on a global level without giving up control of their music or their career.

Over 15 years we have evolved through different phases of the industry — from traditional production to digital distribution, from local releases to international placements. Each phase made us sharper.

Today Terrivol Music operates as a full-service label and music company: we produce, master, distribute, consult and develop artists with the same level of professionalism that major labels offer, but with the agility and artist-first mentality that only an independent can deliver.
