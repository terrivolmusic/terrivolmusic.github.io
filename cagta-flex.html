Urban and trap artist born in Bogotá, Colombia. Founder of Terrivol Music, Cagta Flex has built his career from the foundations of the Colombian independent scene with a sonic identity that fuses contemporary trap with the energy of Latin urban music.

With over 15 years in the music industry, he has released multiple albums and singles that reflect his constant evolution as an artist and producer. Every release is a statement — crafted with full control over production, visuals and strategy.
